EMACS
0 - What is the command to open a file from within Emacs? C-x C-f
1 - What is the command to save a file? C-x C-s
2 - What is the command to cut an entire line? C-k
3 - What is the command to paste? C-y
4 - What is the command to undo? C-x u, C-_ or C-/
5 - What is the command to quit Emacs? C-x C-c
Vi
6 - What is the command to insert text before the cursor, after the cursor? i, a
7 - What is the command to delete and cut the current line? dd
8 - What is the command to undo what you just did? u
9 - What is the command to quit vi even though latest changes have not been saved for this vi call? :q!
10 - What is the command to move the cursor to the start of the current line? ^
11 - What is the command to move the cursor to the end of the line? $
